Admission Process

Admissions at MATS University are based on merit and entrance exams. Follow the steps below to apply:

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents.
  3. Appear for the entrance examination.
  4. Attend the interview (if applicable).
  5. Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat.
