About Us

MATS University, located in Raipur, Chhattisgarh, is a premier institution offering a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. Established with the mission to provide quality education and foster research, MATS University is a hub for students aspiring to excel in various fields.

Courses

We offer a diverse range of courses across multiple disciplines including:

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
  • Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.)
  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)
  • Master of Science (M.Sc.)
  • Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)
  • Master of Arts (M.A.)

Admissions

Admissions are open for the academic year 2024-2025. Prospective students can apply online or visit our campus for more information. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent, ensuring that all eligible candidates have the opportunity to study at MATS University.

Faculty

Our faculty members are experts in their respective fields, bringing a wealth of knowledge and experience to the classroom. They are dedicated to providing high-quality education and mentoring students to achieve their academic and professional goals.
