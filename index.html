About Us

MATS University Raipur is a prestigious institution dedicated to providing quality education and fostering research and innovation. We offer a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs.

Courses

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Bachelor of Science (B.Sc)
